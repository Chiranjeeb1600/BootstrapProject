Consequenses we Face

Several unwanted consequences are faced due to this climate change, live forest wild file, drought, floods and global pandemics. If these overuses continue for few more decades, then it would be difficult to reverse this trend of Climate change and our whole civilization will get destroyed due to these extreme climate events.

Our Current Efforts

Government Effort

Our government is doing their own effort to control this change, whether they are yielding results or are enough is debatable. But we as an inhabitant of this Earth, our home, cannot just wait for these efforts to give desirable output, before it is too late. Every one of us has to do our own bit, be it in a small or a big way, such that we can protect the earth from these bad effects of Climate change
